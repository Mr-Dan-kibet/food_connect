How Food Connect Works

A simple, efficient system that gets food from hotels to families in three easy steps.

Our System

Three Simple Steps

1
🏨

Hotels Report Surplus

Partnered hotels log their surplus meals each day through our simple app or phone call. Our team is notified immediately.

2
🚐

We Collect and Package

Our trained volunteers pick up the food in food-safe containers, ensuring quality and hygiene standards are met at all times.

3
🏗

Delivered to Communities

Meals are distributed to shelters, community kitchens, and families in need - all within hours of collection.

Our Story

About Food Connect

Founded with a simple idea - that no meal should go to waste while people go hungry.

Who We Are

Fighting Hunger,
One Meal at a Time

Food Connect was founded in 2019 by a group of hospitality workers and social workers who saw firsthand how much food was being thrown away every night while shelters nearby ran short. We built a simple bridge between the two worlds.


Today we operate across the city with over 80 volunteers, 180 hotel partners, and have rescued more than 50,000 meals. We believe food is a right, not a privilege.
